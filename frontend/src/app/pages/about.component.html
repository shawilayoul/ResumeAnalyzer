About Resume Analyzer

Resume Analyzer is an AI-powered tool that helps job seekers and professionals evaluate and improve their resumes. By leveraging OpenAI's language models, our system provides instant, personalized feedback to make your resume clearer, more impactful, and better tailored to job roles.

Key Features

  • Automated resume analysis and improvement suggestions
  • Skill match scoring based on job descriptions
  • Highlight of missing keywords and soft skills
  • Grammar and tone analysis

How It Works

Your resume content is processed using OpenAI's natural language processing capabilities. The AI model analyzes the structure, tone, and keyword alignment, and offers suggestions to optimize your resume for hiring systems and recruiters.
